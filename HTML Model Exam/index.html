Edward Kenway

The main character of the game is Edward Kenway

Assassin's Creed IV: Black Flag, like all other Assassin's Creeds, is based on a real time period in human history.

In this case, it's the open seas of the 1700s. As players sail around the Caribbean during the height of piracy (the kind committed with boats, not computers),

they'll encounter several AC: Black Flag characters who are actually real-world historical figures of that era.

Assassin's Creed IV: Black Flag

When AC: Black Flag came out in 2013, the historical and modern-day aspects of the Assassins and Templars were still key pieces of the story. The past-set sections follow pirate and Assassin Edward Kenway as he finds out about the conflict between the two groups. The modern-day timeline takes players to the 21st century as an Abstergo (Templar) agent.
